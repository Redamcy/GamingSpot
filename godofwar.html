About the game

The end of the previous game saw Kratos scattering his wife ashes on Jotunheim, along with his son, Atreus. When they returned to Midgard, Mimir told them that Fimbulwinter already began, triggered when Kratos killed Baldur. God of War: Ragnarok main story begins with the duo trying to survive in the now frigid Midgard. The long winter is nearing its end, and the beginning of the great war Ragnarok is drawing near. Unlike in the previous game, Kratos and Atreus will be able to visit all the nine realms as part of the sequel story, including Vanaheim, Svartalfheim, and Asgard.
